# Attribution & Provenance

This repository is an **independent preservation archive** of the
**Cystic Fibrosis Mutation Database (CFMDB / "CFTR1")**, originally published at
`http://www.genet.sickkids.on.ca/` by **The Hospital for Sick Children
(SickKids), Toronto, Canada** and the CF Genetic Analysis Consortium.

- **Original authors / copyright holders:** The Hospital for Sick Children and
  the contributing investigators listed within each mutation record.
- **Original site last updated:** 25 April 2011.
- **This archive captured on:** 1 July 2026.
- **Purpose:** scholarly preservation of a primary genetics resource that is
  scheduled for retirement, so that the mutation records, submitted phenotypes,
  contributor attributions, and literature linkages remain accessible to the
  cystic fibrosis research community.

All scientific content, mutation records, phenotype descriptions, and
contributor names remain the intellectual property of their original authors and
SickKids. This archive claims **no ownership** over that content and reproduces
it unmodified for preservation and research use. The extraction/build tooling in
`tools/` and the derived index in `data/` are released under the MIT License
(see `LICENSE`).

If you are a rights holder and have questions about this archive, please open an
issue on this repository.
